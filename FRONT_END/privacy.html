Privacy Policy – Safe Space

Last Updated: April 2026

Safe Space ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mental wellness and stress monitoring application that uses physiological sensor data, voice input, and facial expression analysis to provide stress insights and wellness recommendations. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

The app may collect and process the following types of data:

  • Microphone Data: Used for voice analysis and stress detection.
  • Camera Data: Used for facial expression analysis.
  • Bluetooth Sensor Data: Physiological data such as heart rate, electrodermal activity (EDA), temperature, and motion data from wearable sensors.
  • User Input: Text input provided by the user for mood or stress evaluation.
  • Device Information: Basic device and browser information for app functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used only for the following purposes:

  • Stress level detection and analysis
  • Providing AI-based wellness suggestions
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Generating stress analysis reports

3. Data Storage

Safe Space does not permanently store personal physiological data, audio recordings, or facial images. Data is processed temporarily for analysis and then discarded unless the user chooses to save a report.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is used only for analysis within the app.

5. Permissions Used

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Camera – for facial expression analysis
  • Microphone – for voice analysis
  • Bluetooth – for connecting to physiological sensors
  • Internet – for AI processing and report generation

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. All communication between the app and server is encrypted using HTTPS.

7. User Control

You can choose not to use certain features such as camera, microphone, or Bluetooth sensors. You may also uninstall the app at any time to stop all data collection.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
